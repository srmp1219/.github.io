SAP Stories

We wanted to use short videos as a communication medium to deliver information more efficiently and vividly for business purposes. I worked on two of these projects, one for the Data Change Insight project with SAP Analytics Cloud to design a data change video to be pushed to customers, and the other for SAP Business Network to design a dynamic data display banner for the official website.

Role: UX Design, UI Design, Motion Design

Time: 2022.5-2022.10

Tool: Figma HTML

Background


- The way we find, consume, and share information has been changing rapidly.

- Short-form videos have seen a surge in popularity in recent years.

- According to McKinsey, employees spend 1.8 hours every day searching and gathering information(9.3 per week)

Hypothesis


💡 What if complex information/data could automatically generate personalized, audio-visual notifications and be delivered via your mobile/X device？


Goal


The goal is creating a new form of information delivery for enterprise. help users view complex information and analysis anytime, anywhere in a more vivid, simple and fast way.


Project 1--Info video for SAC data change insight （SAP Stories）

Solution

The idea is to automatically synthesize a short video of data from data analytics, text, pictures and other kinds of information. This short video can usually be viewed through a mobile device.
